User Guide

Learn how to use VerifyAI effectively to verify photos and protect yourself online.

Getting Started

Step 1: Upload a Photo

There are two ways to analyze a photo in VerifyAI:

  • From within the app: Tap the upload button and select a photo from your device
  • Share from other apps: Use the "Share" option in messaging apps, email, or web browsers to send photos directly to VerifyAI

Step 2: Wait for Analysis

VerifyAI will automatically begin analyzing the photo using multiple search engines. You'll see progress indicators showing which engines have completed their analysis. Most analyses complete within 10-30 seconds.

Step 3: Review Results

Once analysis is complete, you'll see one of four risk assessments along with detailed information about where the photo was found online.

Understanding Result Details

Source Categories

Results are organized into categories to help you understand where photos were found:

Exact Matches

Photos that are identical or nearly identical to the one you uploaded. These are the most reliable indicators.

Similar Matches

Photos that look similar but may have differences in cropping, filtering, or quality. These should be reviewed carefully.

Source Types

Pay attention to badges indicating where photos were found:

  • Social Media: Found on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc.
  • Stock Photos: Found in commercial photo libraries (HIGH RISK)
  • Web Results: Found on websites, blogs, or other online sources

Priority Sorting

Results are automatically sorted to show the most relevant information first:

  1. Stock photo sources (highest priority - major risk indicator)
  2. Social media sources (high priority - often most relevant)
  3. Other web sources (lower priority)

Best Practices for Staying Safe

Use VerifyAI as Part of a Comprehensive Approach

Photo verification is just one tool. Also consider:

  • Video calls to verify the person matches their photos
  • Asking questions only the real person would know
  • Being suspicious of requests for money or personal information
  • Taking things slowly and building trust over time
  • Trusting your instincts if something feels wrong

Common Red Flags in Romance Scams

Be especially cautious if you notice:

  • Photos that appear too professional or model-like
  • Reluctance to video call or meet in person
  • Quick declarations of love or strong feelings
  • Stories of being in the military, working overseas, or traveling
  • Requests for money, gift cards, or financial help
  • Requests for personal information like bank details
  • Inconsistencies in their story or background

When to Be Most Concerned

Take immediate action if:

  • VerifyAI finds the photo in stock photo libraries
  • The person asks for money or financial information
  • You've never video called or met in person
  • The person makes excuses to avoid verification
  • Multiple red flags are present

Tips for Best Results

Choose the Right Photos

  • Use clear, well-lit photos with visible faces
  • Single-person portraits work best
  • Avoid heavily filtered or edited photos when possible
  • If you have multiple photos, analyze each one separately

What to Do with Results

  • Review all sources, not just the first few
  • Click through to verify sources are legitimate
  • Save or screenshot results for your records
  • Use results as a conversation starter with the person

Privacy and Security

Your photos are never permanently stored. We analyze them and immediately delete the original image data. For complete details, please see our Privacy Policy.

If you have concerns about analyzing a particular photo, you can always choose not to upload it. VerifyAI is designed to help you make informed decisions while respecting your privacy.
