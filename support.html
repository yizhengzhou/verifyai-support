Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use VerifyAI?

Simply upload a photo through the app or share it from another app. VerifyAI will analyze the photo using AI-powered face detection and search across multiple engines. You'll receive results within seconds showing if and where the photo appears online.

How does VerifyAI protect my privacy?

We take privacy very seriously. Your photos are only temporarily stored during the analysis process and are permanently deleted immediately after analysis completes. We never archive, backup, or permanently store any of your uploaded images. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

How long does analysis take?

Most analyses complete within 10-30 seconds, depending on the complexity of the photo and current server load. You'll see real-time progress updates as different search engines complete their analysis.

How do I interpret the results?

VerifyAI provides one of four risk assessments:

  • High Risk: Photo found in commercial stock photo libraries
  • Suspicious: Photo appears in multiple exact matches across different sources
  • Uncertain: Similar photos found, but may be coincidental
  • Clean: No matches found in any search engine

For detailed guidance, visit our User Guide.

Why does the app need certain permissions?

VerifyAI requests the following permissions:

  • Photo Library Access: To allow you to select photos for analysis
  • Camera Access: To take new photos for immediate analysis (optional)

We only access photos you explicitly select or take within the app. We never access your entire photo library without your permission.

What if the analysis shows my photo is being used elsewhere?

If VerifyAI finds your photo being used by someone else, this could indicate:

  • Someone may have copied your photo from your social media
  • The photo was shared without your knowledge
  • In some cases, it could be a false positive

We recommend reviewing the sources carefully and considering whether you want to:

  • Contact the platform where the photo appears
  • Report unauthorized use
  • Adjust your privacy settings on social media

Remember: VerifyAI provides information to help you make informed decisions. The final decision is always yours.

Can I analyze multiple photos at once?

Currently, VerifyAI analyzes one photo at a time to ensure accurate results and maintain fast processing speeds. We may add batch analysis in future updates.

What types of photos work best?

VerifyAI works best with:

  • Clear, well-lit photos with visible faces
  • Single-person portraits
  • Photos where the face is not obscured

Photos with multiple people, low quality, or heavily filtered images may produce less accurate results.

Is my data shared with third parties?

To provide our service, we temporarily share photos with trusted analysis providers (Google Cloud Vision, SerpAPI, Shutterstock) for the duration of the analysis only. These services are bound by their own privacy policies. We never sell your data or use it for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

How accurate is VerifyAI?

VerifyAI uses multiple industry-leading search engines and AI technologies to provide comprehensive results. However, no system is 100% accurate. Results should be considered as one factor in your decision-making process, not the only factor.

Will you add more features in the future?

Yes! We're continuously working to improve VerifyAI. Planned features include:

  • Additional search engines for broader coverage
  • AI-generated image detection
  • Deepfake video detection
  • Region-specific search capabilities

We appreciate your feedback and use it to make VerifyAI better for everyone.
